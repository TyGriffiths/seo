SEO Best Practices

SEO can be increased through keyword frequency, weight, proximity, prominence, and placement. Search engines have rules that, if followed, improve your SEO. Abiding by these rules is called "white-hat" SEO, while trying to cheat them is called "black-hat" SEO.

An important meta tag that may need to be used is meta name="robots" content="noindex,nofollow", which will tell search engines not to search through your website.
